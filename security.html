Data Security & Privacy

At ClarityKey, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We believe that what you write belongs to you alone, and our infrastructure is built to respect that boundary.

1. Safe AI Processing

The text you process using ClarityKey is transmitted securely over encrypted connections to our premium AI models purely for real-time correction. We absolutely do not store, log, or use your personal data to train our own models. Once the text is corrected, it disappears from our memory.

2. Secure Infrastructure

Our backend authentications and databases are built around Supabase, providing enterprise-grade security. Your login credentials, email addresses, and subscription statuses are encrypted and protected by advanced industry-standard security protocols.

3. Safe Payments

We partner with Stripe, a world-class payment processor trusted by millions of companies, to handle your transactions securely. ClarityKey never touches, views, or stores your credit card information on our own servers.
