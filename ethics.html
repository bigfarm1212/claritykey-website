Our Ethics & Mission

Empowering Every Writer

ClarityKey AI was built on a fundamental ethical belief: technology should make life easier, not more complex. Our mission is to completely remove the friction from written communication, especially for individuals navigating dyslexia. We are building an inclusive world where no one feels hesitant to express their thoughts.

Transparent & Helpful AI

We believe in using AI merely as a tool for personal empowerment, not for mass surveillance or algorithmic tracking. Our AI models act exclusively as your personal proofreader. We only intervene exactly when you ask us to, ensuring the author's true voice is always preserved.

Accessible Software

Accessibility tools are often locked behind steep paywalls. We have made a principled commitment to forever provide a free tier so that basic, necessary spelling support remains available to anyone who needs it, regardless of their financial situation.
